About Reedy

Not a career politician.
Your neighbor.

Reedy Berg grew up in the Yakima Valley, graduated from Eisenhower High School, and earned his degree from Gonzaga University where he pitched for the baseball program. After years in the fruit industry, he chose to give back by becoming an educator.

Since 2016, Reedy has taught history and civics at Toppenish Middle School. He holds a Master's in Education from Heritage University. In 2024, the community elected him to Yakima City Council, where he now serves as Deputy Mayor.

Reedy and his wife have two young children and family roots in the Valley stretching back 40+ years. He knows what's at stake here because he lives it.
